How to talk to LLMs

## What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt engineering refers to the process of crafting instructions that guide an AI model to generate the desired output. It is one of the most common model adaptation techniques and does not require changing a model's weights, unlike fine-tuning. Instead, it leverages the model's inherent capabilities through well-structured prompts.
