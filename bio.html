Mahir Yavuz

Mahir is the Head of Engineering for Search, Recs, Ads at Etsy and former Co-founder and CTO of Topos.

With over 20 years of experience in product development, data science, AI/ML, he has led teams to build products for companies like Nike, PwC, Google, Microsoft, and more.

Mahir’s work has been featured in international exhibitions and festivals, including the Ars Electronica Festival, SIGGRAPH, the Istanbul Biennial, and ISEA. He is a sought-after speaker, delivering talks and workshops at events such as the Microsoft Build, Visualized, TEDx Istanbul and Vienna, and for organizations like NYC Government’s Center for Innovation through Data Intelligence.

An experienced educator since 2003, Mahir has taught at institutions like the University of Pennsylvania, Pratt, and the University of Art and Design Linz, advising numerous student projects and guest lecturing at MIT, NYU, Columbia, and more.
